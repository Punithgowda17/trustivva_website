About Trustivva

Trustivva Solutions LLC is a U.S.-based provider of title research and property reporting services for title companies, underwriters, and real estate professionals nationwide. Headquartered in Wisconsin, the company delivers accurate, timely, and dependable results built on verified data, secure handling, and clear communication — ensuring confidence in every transaction.

Our Mission

To deliver precise, dependable, and transparent title research solutions that strengthen client confidence and support seamless real estate transactions nationwide.

Our Services

Current Owner Search

Verifies the current owner vested on title and identifies all open mortgages, recorded liens, and judgments against the property.

Two Owner Search

Examines title history for the last two owners, including deeds, mortgages, liens, and taxes.

Pre-Foreclosure Search

Provides ownership and lien details to support pre-foreclosure and loss mitigation activities.

Full Search

Covers complete chain of title, including mortgages, liens, judgments, and tax delinquencies.

Update / Datedown Search

Identifies newly recorded documents or changes since the last title search.

Assignment Verification Report

Outlines mortgage details and confirms the assignment chain to determine the current beneficiary.
